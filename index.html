Circuit builder

Drag a gate onto any qubit at any time step, or click a gate then click a cell. For CNOT / CZ / SWAP, place it on the control qubit, then click the target qubit in the same column — a cyan line connects control and target. Click a placed rotation (RX/RY/RZ) to set its angle; right-click a gate to remove it. Use the cyan measure button next to a qubit to choose which qubits to read out — measured qubits turn green and are highlighted everywhere. If you select none, all qubits are shown.
