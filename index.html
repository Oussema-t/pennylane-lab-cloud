Circuit builder

Drag a gate onto any qubit at any time step, or click a gate then click a cell. For CNOT / CZ / SWAP, place it on the control qubit, then click the target qubit in the same column — a line connects control and target. Drop the Measure gate on the qubits you want to read out; if you place none, all qubits are shown. Click a placed rotation (RX/RY/RZ) to set its angle; right-click a gate to remove it.
